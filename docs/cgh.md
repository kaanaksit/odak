Odak contains essential ingredients for research and development targeting Computer-Generated Holography.

In the rest of this document, you will find engineering notes and relevant functions in Odak that help you describe the complex nature of light on a computer.
Note that, the creators of this documentation are from the `Computational Displays` domain, however the provided submodules can potentially aid other lines of research as well, such as `Computational Imaging` or `Computational Microscopy` ðŸŒŸ.
